1.	Выведите перечень сумм заказов продавца Rifkin.

SELECT o.amt
FROM Orders o, Salespeople s
WHERE s.snum = o.snum AND s.sname = 'Rifkin';

SELECT amt
FROM Orders
WHERE snum = 
    (SELECT snum
    FROM Salespeople
    WHERE sname = 'Rifkin'
    );
